Planning to Visit Us?

Our secretariat welcomes all members and prospective members. Here's how to make the most of your visit.

1

Schedule in advance

Drop us a WhatsApp or email so the right team lead is ready for you — walk-ins are welcome but appointments are served first.

2

Bring your student ID

We'll need to verify your Wajir County student status for membership registration and scholarship documentation.

3

Come during secretariat hours

Monday to Saturday — see the hours panel for exact times. We're closed Sundays and public holidays.
