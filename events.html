History

Our Journey So Far

Every event we've hosted has shaped the community we are today. Here's a look back at WCSA milestones since founding.

March 2023

WCSA Founding Assembly

Over 80 students gathered in Nairobi to formally establish the Wajir County Students Association, elect founding leadership, and adopt the organisation's first constitution.

80 attendees Nairobi
August 2023

Inaugural Annual Summit

WCSA's first Annual Summit brought together 150 students and 20 alumni for two days of workshops, career panels, and cultural performances. A milestone for the young organisation.

170 attendees Nairobi
January 2024

Mentorship Nexus Launch

The first cohort of 40 student-alumni mentorship pairs were matched and celebrated at a launch event in Nairobi. The programme has since grown to 120+ pairings.

40 pairs Nairobi
August 2024

Annual Summit 2024 — Record Attendance

250 members attended the second Annual Summit — the largest WCSA gathering to date. Keynote speakers included alumni from government, tech, and law who spoke on career building in Kenya.

FAQs

Event Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before registering for a WCSA event.

Do I need to be a WCSA member to attend?

Most WCSA events are open to all Wajir County students, but members get priority registration, discounted fees, and exclusive access to some workshops. Joining is free.

How do I register for an event?

Click "Register" on any event card to fill out the registration form. You'll receive a confirmation email with event details and payment instructions where applicable.

Are event fees refundable?

Paid event registrations are refundable up to 7 days before the event date. Contact events@wcsa.co.ke with your registration details to request a refund.

Will events be recorded or streamed?

Selected events — especially keynote sessions — are recorded and shared in the WCSA members' portal within two weeks of the event. Workshops are not typically recorded to encourage participation.

Can I volunteer at WCSA events?

Yes! WCSA events are largely volunteer-run. Members can apply to join event crews through the Events Committee. Volunteers receive free entry and recognition at the event.

How can my organisation sponsor a WCSA event?

We welcome sponsorship from organisations aligned with student development. Reach out via partnerships@wcsa.co.ke for our sponsorship prospectus and available packages.

Be the First to Hear About New Events

WCSA members get early-bird access, discounted tickets, and exclusive invites to closed workshops and networking sessions. Membership is free — always.
