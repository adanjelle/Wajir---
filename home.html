Who We Are

Built on Purpose,
Driven by Community

WCSA exists to ensure that every Wajir County student in higher education has the support, network, and opportunities they deserve.

Our Mission

To unite Wajir County students in higher education by fostering academic excellence, leadership development, and cultural pride through mentorship, networking, and strategic partnerships that create real-world impact.

Our Vision

A generation of empowered, visionary leaders from Wajir County who drive positive change in Kenya and beyond — excelling in their fields while uplifting each other through unity, shared purpose, and unwavering cultural pride.
