From Wajir to Campuses Nationwide — Our Journey

Wajir County Students Association (WCSA) was founded in 2023 with a bold vision: to unite students from Wajir studying across Kenyan universities and colleges. Today, we are a vibrant family of 500+ active members spread across 12+ institutions, fostering mentorship, leadership camps, career expos, and cultural galas that celebrate our rich heritage.

Our association thrives on collaboration — we host networking forums, peer-to-peer tutoring, and alumni mentorship circles that have helped countless students navigate campus life, secure internships, and build lifelong bonds. We don't just create events; we cultivate future changemakers.

From charity drives in Wajir County to university-wide leadership summits, WCSA stands for progress, unity, and academic excellence. Our story is still being written — and every new member adds a powerful verse.

Our Mission

To unite Wajir County students across all Kenyan universities and colleges by fostering academic excellence, leadership development, cultural pride, and strategic partnerships that create sustainable community impact.

Our Vision

A generation of empowered, visionary leaders from Wajir County who drive positive change, excel globally, and uplift their communities through unity, integrity, and lifelong service.
