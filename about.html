Our Story

From Wajir to Campuses Nationwide

The Wajir County Students Association (WCSA) was founded in 2023 with a single bold idea — that students from Wajir, scattered across Kenya's universities, deserved a home away from home. A place to belong, to grow, and to give back.

"We don't just create events — we cultivate future changemakers who carry Wajir's legacy forward."

From a handful of students at one university, we've grown into a vibrant association of 500+ active members across 12+ institutions. Leadership camps, career expos, peer tutoring, and alumni mentorship have helped countless students navigate campus life and forge lifelong bonds.

From charity drives in Wajir County to university-wide summits, WCSA stands for progress, unity, and the belief that where you come from is a strength. Our story is still being written — and every member adds a powerful verse.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

Every chapter has been a stepping stone toward a stronger, more united Wajir community.

January 2023

WCSA Is Founded

Visionary Wajir students at the University of Nairobi formalize the association, elect the first executive committee, and launch the founding charter.

April 2023

Inaugural Cultural Gala

Over 200 students attend WCSA's first Cultural Night — celebrating Wajir heritage through music, poetry, and traditional attire. An annual tradition is born.

August 2023

Multi-Campus Expansion

WCSA expands to 6 universities including Kenyatta University, Moi University, and Strathmore — establishing chapter coordinators at each institution.

February 2024

First Leadership Summit

300+ attendees gather for the inaugural WCSA Leadership Summit, featuring keynotes from prominent Wajir alumni in government, business, and academia.

2024 – Present

500+ Members & Growing

WCSA surpasses 500 active members across 12+ institutions and launches its flagship mentorship program connecting students with 25+ professional alumni.

Purpose

Mission & Vision

The guiding principles behind every decision, event, and connection we forge.

Our Mission

To unite Wajir County students across all Kenyan universities by fostering academic excellence, leadership development, cultural pride, and strategic partnerships that create sustainable community impact.

  • Peer tutoring and academic support networks
  • Career expos and professional development workshops
  • Community outreach and charity initiatives in Wajir
  • Scholarship and financial aid access support

Our Vision

A generation of empowered, visionary leaders from Wajir County who drive positive change, excel globally, and uplift their communities through unity, integrity, and lifelong service.

  • A WCSA chapter in every major Kenyan university
  • Wajir graduates leading in every professional sector
  • A sustainable alumni network investing back in Wajir
  • Kenya's most impactful county student association
Core Values

The Principles That Guide Us

Our values are not words on a wall — they are the standards we hold ourselves to every single day.

Unity & Solidarity

We rise together. Every member is family, and we support each other through every challenge and every milestone.

Academic Excellence

We champion learning and intellectual growth as the foundation of our community's long-term progress.

Integrity

We lead with honesty, transparency, and moral courage. Trust is the bedrock of everything we build together.

Community Service

We give back through impactful outreach and volunteer drives that tangibly improve lives in Wajir County.

Innovation

We embrace fresh ideas and creative problem-solving — the courage to challenge the status quo for the better.

Cultural Pride

We honor our Wajir heritage — language, traditions, identity — as a source of strength, not limitation.
