How We Govern Ourselves

WCSA operates on three democratic principles — every position is elected, every decision is transparent, and every member has a voice.

Democratic Elections

All executive and directorate positions are filled through a free and fair election held every two years. Any WCSA member in good standing is eligible to run for office.

Published Constitution

Our governance is guided by a written constitution adopted by the founding members in 2023. It is reviewed and updated at each Annual General Meeting.

Annual General Meeting

The AGM is the highest decision-making body in WCSA. All members are invited to attend, vote on resolutions, and hold leadership to account each year.

Interested in Leading WCSA?

The next election cycle opens in 2025. Any WCSA member in good standing can run for any position. Learn about the requirements and start your journey in leadership.
